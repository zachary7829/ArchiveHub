1.2.1: (160 Actions) UltraUpdate 1.2.1 Provides important stability and speed improvements and is recommended to all users of UltraUpdate.

1.2: (162 Actions) Introducing UltraUpdate 1.2, a revolutionary update. You know UltraUpdate has insanely good and useful features - that’s why in this update, I decided to improve it’s features even more, with Fully-Worded Version Support, a native Dark Theme, and the ability to transfer UltraUpdate data to Swing Updater. But features aren’t the only thing this update brings to the table. Stability is very important as well. That’s why UltraUpdate now uses 0Masking, so not only do you have the ability to put in worded versions, but also increase stability by fixing some nasty auto-formatting bugs with the shortcuts app. Lastly, we know you want and care about speed, and UltraUpdate already has that covered. However, this time, I made UltraUpdate the fastest it’s ever been, at a mere 162 actions. That’s more than 100 less than UpdateKit, which doesn’t even have a CFU Engine integrated! Speed. Stability. Features. That’s UltraUpdate.
