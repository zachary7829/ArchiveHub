Description

The oldest iOS 12 version is located here : https://routinehub.co/download/8809

A launcher to use from your Home Screen.

  • Choose Background colours
  • Scrollable so you can fit more on the screen.
  • Have any or all types of launcher.
  • Asks you to select a photo for contacts that aren't available.
  • Shortcuts can be ordered, or edited.
  • Facetime calls easily identified
  • Markdown notes
  • Icons - Photos, App Store, or files (.icns .png) (keep a folder of your favorite icons on iCloud), or use Quick Icon Maker to make your icons https://routinehub.co/shortcut/1090
