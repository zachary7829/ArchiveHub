3.0 1 year, 7 months ago
iOS 12
Merry Christmas & Happy Holidays! I’ve been working on this for quite awhile and I’m happy to announce that the 3.0 update is here!

Do Not Disturb 3.0: The Locations Update

1. Locations
I finally implemented multiple locations with the shortcut! Sadly, there’s only 1 additional location, but soon enough, I will add 3 more, for a total of FIVE locations for you guys to enjoy. Here’s what I did.
- Added a new Locations option to add New, Edit, or View locations
- If you try to add a third one, it won’t let you, since I’m trying to implement three additional locations.
- New folder management to separate the main files from the locations
- Choose to run from a location when clicking “Run Shortcut”
- many more!

2. Menu Changes
- New “Location Option”
- New “Turn on DND Manually” for those just wanting to turn on Do Not Disturb and not go through the silly location add-ons
- Emojis added to be creative

3. Misc
- Bug Fixes
- Improvements all around the shortcut
- Normal Mode has been disabled as I prep for a new “Normal” DnD management, will come back in a later update.
- Removed Support for Things in “Remind Me”

2.0 1 year, 9 months ago
iOS 12
UpdateKit:
- Check Version without hassle of prompting for new updates while running the shortcut, so you can get right into it!
- Check Version is a Menu Option. Click on it and it will run UpdateKit. Check my website as well for updates.

Functions:
1. Numbers
- Plug in a number (12.25) and it will convert to (12:25 AM)
- Only works up until the same date @ 11:59 PM
2. Date & Time
- Redirects you to the Shortcuts App to prompt you the date and time.
- Only works up until TOMORROWS date @ 11:59 PM

Misc:
- Minor Improvements
- Added prompt to alert you whenever Do Not Disturb is disabled.
Current Supported Apps:
1. Apple Reminders
2. Wunderlist
3. Things
Things will have to be configured on its own until I get the app for my devices, which won’t be for awhile.
- Go to Website! Go to my website to check out my other shortcuts, as well as this one.
- Choose Option for Google or Chrome
- New Changelog mode to check out the changes
- Choose between URL, Alert (Long), or Alert (Brief)
- Shortcut renamed to Do Not Disturb from Do Not Disturb @ School

Known Issues:
- Going over the Numbers time limit (13.xx) will pass through to your normal Do Not Disturb time and not alert you
- Same goes with Date & Time.
