Description

A All-In-One Downloader for many websites including YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, Dailymotion, Instagram, TikTok, SoundCloud, and Vimeo.

All YouTube videos can be downloaded in up to 8K video quality and in 320kbps audio quality, making this one of the first shortcuts being able to do this. It can also download music videos from YouTube as well.
