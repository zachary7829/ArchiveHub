0.7.6 7 months ago
iOS 13
Improved: Shortcuts are now tracked with their RoutineHub IDs instead of their names. This should allow you to change the names of your shortcuts without losing track of the counts. IMPORTANT: Your shortcuts will automatically be re-added to the database the first time this update is run.
Improved: Reliability when running with voice. The shortcut now asks you if you want to try again if it fails to retrieve user data.
New: Heart counts are displayed in the "More about this user" menu. The shortcut does not track heart counts in the database yet. Meaning, it won't notify you of new hearts.
(Thanks to @tctalk for the feedback)
