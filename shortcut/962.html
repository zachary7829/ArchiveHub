Cookie Clicker

Cookie Clicker Classic recreated in Shortcuts!

Cookie Clicker is a Shortcut inspired from one of my other Shortcuts, Clicker. The goal of this Shortcut is to recreate the Cookie Clicker Classic game by Orteil that can be found on Orteil's website.

Cookie Clicker is an incremental/idle/clicker game about collecting cookies. You can use these cookies to buy buildings from the store, which automatically make more cookies for you. The ultimate goal is to collect as many cookies as you possibly can!

Features:
  • Shop with many buildings, including cursors, farms, and portals!
  • Progression is made even while you are not playing the game!
  • Fun cookie facts!
  • Runs fully from widget mode!
  • Save management system to export, import, and delete your saves!
  • Emojis!
  • UpdateKit and DependKit support!
