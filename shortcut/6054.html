Description

AnonyGram and Download

AnonyGram and Download is a shortcut that allows you to follow public Instagram profiles anonymously and download their stories and posts easily.



Private

It's made with privacy in mind. The shortcut uses the built-in webview, so no browser history. The list of accounts you follow are encoded so spotlight won't show the usernames. If you want to delete everything, it's a press away.





Password Protected

You can also password protect the shortcut using a PIN of your choice. No one without iOS Shortcuts expertise will be able to see who you follow.





Add from Instagram

Follow new accounts on AnonyGram directly on Instagram! Simply press “share” on a post of the person you want to follow, scroll down until you find AnonyGram. Confirm your intent and it’s done!



Easy management

You can easily follow and unfollow accounts.






iCloud Sync

If you use Shortcuts with iCloud, all of your settings (including pin) and accounts you follow will keep synced!

No third party Updater Shortcut required.

AnonyGram and Download has an integrated update mechanism called Swing Updater[E]. Thanks to the embedded updater, this shortcut does not require any external updater shortcut. The shortcut checks for updates itself once per day, so you'll always be up to date and never miss an update.
