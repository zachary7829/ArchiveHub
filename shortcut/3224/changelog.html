2.6 9 months, 3 weeks ago
iOS 13
- Your device’s current volume is now retrieved at the beginning of the shortcut and used to set the volume back to the EXACT value as it was before. The volume is set to 50% when playing a sound selected, then back to what it was before hand. Credits go to @tctalk for the suggestion (1 month, 2 weeks ago).

- NOTE: No files are saved to your iCloud Drive using the method I’ve used, as it is not needed. It’s simple as Getting the current volume, setting it to a variable, and retrieving that as a magic variable for another set volume action.
