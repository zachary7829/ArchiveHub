Scheduled Job

This "subflow" shortcut will schedule your shortcut to be ran daily at the time of your choosing.

Description

This shortcut works as a "subflow". Set it up in two easy steps: 1. On your primary Shortcut, create a Dictionary action with "name" (main Shortcut name) and "time" (example: 7:00 PM). 2. pass it into a Run Shortcut activity with Scheduled Job selected. That's it!
