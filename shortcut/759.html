Description

With PhotoCollage Pro you can create photo collages like a pro! You’ve got all possible options to choose from: Grid (w/ or w/o Gap), Horizontal (w/ and w/o Gap) and Vertical (w/ and w/o Gap).

Import your images from Photos or Files. Select between Grid, Horizontal or Vertical layout or all these options with a gap. If you decide to select an option without a gap, your images will be automatically arranged. If you’ve chosen the gap option you will be prompted to insert the width of the gap. Next your collage will be created, you can save it (Photos, Files, Dropbox etc.), send it (AirDrop, iMessage, WhatsApp or Mail) or share/post it on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter or WordPress.

Please keep in mind: If you want a grid based collage, choose an even number of images. They will be cropped to fit the collage. If you choose horizontal or vertical layout, there will be no cropping—all images will be simply arranged next to each other (w/ or w/o a gap).
