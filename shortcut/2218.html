Description

Project notes

This is a full-featured transport Shortcut designed to be good enough to live in your dock and be your first port of call when you need to get from A to B.

New Addition: Favourite Locations:

Features

  • Choose to use either Apple Maps, Google Maps or Citymapper, Transit or HERE WeGo (Waze is also available when you select driving)
  • Choose to get home or to work with two taps
  • NEW Save favourite locations for quick access
  • Get directions to upcoming appointments in your calendar
  • Find nearby cafes, shops, restaurants, petrols stations, parks etc
  • Get directions to a selected contact
  • Choose from various transport modes: walking, cycling, driving, taxi or public transport
  • Get your directions sent across to either the Uber or Lyft apps to book a taxi quickly
  • Save favourite contacts for quick access
  • With the press of a button, let your loved one know you’re on the way home with an ‘on my way’ text with a choice of customisable, canned messages along with a map of your current location and an eta home
  • Share your current location - either as a map link or grab the nearest address
  • Detects addresses on your clipboard or from a Share Sheet input and offers to find directions to the address
  • A fluid, intuitive experience, designed to ask for information when needed rather than a lengthy initial setup process
  • UpdateKit enabled

Fun facts: Get Me There has around 1400 action blocks and was built entirely on a iPhone XS
