Description

With Safari Auto Scroll you can read on the web hands-free. Choose an automatic scroll speed or define it yourself and read articles on the web without annoying manual scrolling.

 
 
 
 

 
 
 

 
 
 

Instructions

  1. Open Safari and visit a website of your choice
  2. Open the Share Sheet
  3. Tap Safari Auto Scroll
  4. Follow the instructions and allow access

 
 
 
 

Video Demonstration (iOS 12 Version)

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

No third party Updater Shortcut required.

Safari Auto Scroll has an integrated update mechanism called Embed-a-Update. Thanks to the embedded updater, Safari Auto Scroll does not require any external updater shortcut. The shortcut checks for updates itself, so you'll always be up to date and never miss an update.
