4.001 1 month, 3 weeks ago
iOS 13
Introducing Super Respring 4.0. This is a major update to Super Respring. First, you know Super Respring has outstanding software support, being the only respring shortcut with support for 13.4 and 13.4.1. I’d like to announce that 4.0 outdoes what’s already the best and makes it better by adding support for 13.5-13.5.1, being the first version of Super Respring to support all current firmwares at launch. We’ve also redone the settings menu to make the icons look more appealing for you, and have also added a credits section featuring amazing people that have helped during development. We’ve also redone the Jailbreak menu to auto-use a bug instead of SSH if you’re on 13.0-13.5.1, and we’ve also made icons for it to make this menu look absolutely outstanding and fit Super Respring’s high quality. We also made the user able to choose what bug they want to respring with, and we use a beautiful vCard menu for it. This is a major update to Super Respring, and one of the biggest yet.
