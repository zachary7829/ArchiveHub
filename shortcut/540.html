Description

Tilt Scroll

Share a web page from Safari to this Shortcut and it will allow you to tilt your device towards you or away from you to scroll up and down within the viewport.

Most iPhones shouldn't have a problem. Glitchy on my iPad Air.

With this initial release, refresh the page to stop the script.
