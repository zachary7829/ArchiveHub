Description

Welcome to I need a lifeline! This is a Shortcut designed for those struggling with the difficult things in life (i.e. Depression, Anxiety, Thoughts of Suicide, etc.) and need someone to lean on for support. This Shortcut provides resources and numbers for you to contact should you need them. Of course, you can add your own if you need to. Upon setup, this Shortcut will prompt you to select contacts from your own address book to talk to (think of it as your own personal support system). When creating your own Safety Plan, please feel free to redo it as many times as you need!

This Shortcut is not designed to diagnose or treat any type of illness and any way, shape, or form! If you are in danger of hurting yourself or someone else, call 911!
