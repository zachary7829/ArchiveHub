Version 2.00: Now with both 1500 and 2500 league searches available and adjustable from top 1 through top 100 IVs (change your default via the Customize Shortcut in settings), with much, much faster searching mechanisms, and input the number, the name or a stat to find your Pokémon.

This shortcut searches your chosen Pokémon for CP and HP matches to the top 1-100 cp-1500 and cp-2500 League IVs.

No longer bulky! It searches first for your starting Pokémon stats, then for your final Pokémon stats, and then copies the Top Pvp choices CP&HP search string directly to your clipboard.
