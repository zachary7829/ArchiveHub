Hi. Great work! Just wanted to recommend a change. I suggest you use either UpdateHub or Check For Updates instead of UpdateKit, they will make your app a lot faster. Your current integration of UpdateKit requires it to be placed at the start, which in your case is necessary but isn't recommended normally as it adds time to the Shortcut. Usually you place UpdateKit at the end of your Shortcut so everything can run first. If you use either updater I mentioned you won't have this issue.
