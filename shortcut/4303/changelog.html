What's new in v.2.0?

[New Features]

• Introducing: "Topics"
-> In Topics, you can easily track news topics with a keyword. Simply add the topic (or keyword) to the Shorty News topic list. Shorty News then searches through the five current articles per source based on your keyword.
You'll then get the latest news with the topic from all the sources available in your region. This way you always stay up-to-date and well informed.

• Introducing: "Embed-a-Update X Shortycuts"
Another step into the future.
-> With the redesigned 'Embed-a-Update'-Updater by Shortycuts and ROP, Shorty News is becoming more independent. This updater has basic options like Embed-a-Update. It's more design-oriented to make your experience of Shorty News even better and more comfortable.

• Introducing: "ROP's Design"
The wonderful menu design by ROP has been transformed to Shorty News


[Reader]

Introducing: Shorty News's Reader tools
"Reader tools" offers you the opportunity to:
• change your font size when you're using the Shorty News's Reader
• change the theme for the article you read temporarily

Improvements
• You have the opportunity to save the article after you reading it
